Monkeys

My favorite animal is one that is closes to humans...you guessed it Monkeys. Monkeys are known for their cool kid attitude.

  • Did you know? Monkey's groom each other as away to build relationship bond.
  • Did you know? Monkey's have a very diverse diet some eat up to 50 different species of fruit in a single day.
  • Did you know? Monkey's prefer to eat fruit when it is ripe from January to May, and ate more leaves from June to December.
